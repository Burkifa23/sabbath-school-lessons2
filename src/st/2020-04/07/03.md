---
title:  Ba li Bolelle Bana ba Bona
date:  09/11/2020
---

Lipesalema tsa Testamenteng ea Khale li bonahala li qetella li bapala karolo e itseng maphelong a tumelo ea Baiseraele ba khale. Li ne li phetoa ka hlooho, li binoa, hangata ka liletsa, nakong ea khumamelo, haholo-holo khumamelo ea sechaba ka moka, eo ka Testamenteng ea khale e neng e le khubu ea kamoo batho ba khumamelang kateng. Iseraele e ne e sebetsa e le sechaba, e le sechaba, e n’e khumamela ’moho. Lipesalema haele hantle ke lithothokisao, Tehillîm, le bolela lipina tsa thoriso.” ’Me ha re binela Molimo lithoriso, eng le eng eo re e etsang, re khumamela Morena.

`Bala Lipesaleme 78:1–17. Molaetsa oa bohlokoa ke ofe moo, ’me o nyallana joang le taba ee eohle ea thuto le khumamelo?`

Hona le sepheo se itseng ka molaetsa oa Pesalema ea 78. Temaneng ea 2, Asafe o bontša kamoo re tla arolelana “lithuto tse lefifi tsa khale.” Lentsoe lefifi ha le bolele ntho’e

“tšabehang” empa, ntho’e lerootho, kapa e felang, joalokaha histori e ba joalo ha nako e ntse e-ea. Liphetolelong tse ling, lefifi le nkoa e le “lekunutu (NLV) kapa “li’nete tsa khale tse monate” (Message). Taba mona ke hore, sohle se neng se kenyelatsa thuto ea Isearele, e ne keneyeletsa ho ruta bana lipale tsa kamoo Morena a sebetsanang le sechaba se khethiloeng kateng.

`Sheba Lipesaleme 78:6–17. Lithuto tse ikhethileng tseo ba neng ba tlameha ho li ruta bana ba bona ke life? Sepheo sa moshoelella sa thuto eee ne le sefe?`

`Hara lipakane tsa thuto, joalokaha ho bonoa temaneng, ke hore bana ba tla ithuta ho tsepa Molimo le ho boloka mealao ea hae. Ke joang temana e kang ea Tsenolo 14:12 e ka re thusang ho re bontsa taba eona eo kajeno?`
