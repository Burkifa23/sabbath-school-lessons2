### Fundela Isifundo sale Veki
Daniel 3, IsiTy. 14:6–12, Nd. 78:1–17, Yohane 4:7–26, 1 IZikr. 16:1–36, Marko 7:1–13.

> <p>Indima Yenkumbulo</p>
> “Mnikeni uYehova uzuko lwegama lakhe. Zisani umnikelo, nime phambi kwakhe. Mnquleni uYehova, nivethe ezingcwele” (1 IziKronike 16:29).

Unqulo yinxalenye yoluntu, inxalenye yendalo yomntu, yona loo ndalo iwileyo. Ngaphandle kombuzo, sadala saba ngabantu abathi, ngenkululeko asinike yona uThixo, banqule uYehova kuba simthanda kwaye sisazi ukuba ufanele ukunqulwa. Unqulo olunjalo kunokuba lwalulula kakhulu kwihlabathi laphambi kokuWa, apho abantu babekwazi ukuhlangana noThixo ubuso ngobuso bengonakaliswanga sisono, ukuba nentshabalalo—indalo thina esiyaziyo ukuba kuphela komhlaba owileyo onokuyicinga luzizi.

Namhlanje, phofu, nangona into engaphakathi edinga ukunqula isekhona, ithe, njengayo nayiphi into kulo mhlaba, yakrunwa yajijwa sisono, nto leyo ethetha ukuba ezinye izinto thina, njengezidalwa ezinqulayo, zingaphela zingamnquli uYehova ngendlela amele ukunqulwa ngayo (bona, umzekelo, kuMarko 7:1–13, Yer. 7:4). Kungoko, ngenxa yokuba unqulo lusembindini kumava wethu, imfundo yobuKristu kufuneka isebenze ngombandela wonqulo, umxholo wesifundo sale veki.

_*Funda esi sifundo ukulungiselela iSabatha kaNovemba 14._
