---
title:  Ukufunda Ingqondo YeTitshala EyiNkosi
date:  27/10/2020
---

`Yintoni ehlupha ingqondo kaPawulos ngosapho lwamaKristu aseFiliphi ngexesha ebhala incwadi eya kuwo? Filipi 2:1–4; Filipi 4:2, 3.`

Iindima ezikwabaseFilipi 2:1-11 ziyeyona ndawo entsonkothe kakhulu kuyo yonke iBhayibhile. Ithetha ngobukho bukaKristu kwangaphambi kokuba azalwe emhlabeni, ubuThixo baKhe, ukuba ngumntu kwaKhe, ubuntu baKhe, ukwamkela kwaKhe ukufa emnqamlezweni. Ichaza indlela ende ehlayo awayihambayo uYesu ukusuka ezulwini ukuya eKalvari (Filipi 2:5–8). Ikwachaza indlela uYise awamphakamisa ngayo uYesu embeka kwisikhundla sokuba anqulwe yindalo yonke (Filipi 2:9–11). Ininzi inyaniso efunjwe kwezo ndima.

`UPawulos uyiveza njani intetho ekwabaseFilipi 2:5–11? Kwiziganeko zobomi bukaYesu azibabazayo, zeziphi wena ocinga ukuba unga amakholwa angazibonakalalisa kobawo ubomi? Filipi 2:6–11.`

UPawulos uthembe ukuba amakholwa aseFilipi, anokuba ayethanda ukuphikisa, aya kufunda kuYesu nasekubeni abe yinyama kwaKhe. Ukuba uYesu wamkela imo yomntu—“ubume bekhoboka, ezelwe ngokufana nomntu” (Filipi 2:7, NRSV)—ade avume ukubethelelwa emnqamlezweni, kubekephi kubo ukulindeleka ngaphezulu ukuba bazithobe omnye komnye nalapho beqhutywa luthando? Siyakhunjuzwa ukuba kuninzi okunokufundwa kwiTitshala eyiNkosi, uYesu. Siyafunda kwizigidimi ukuba uyabelana kulungiselelo lwaKhe lwasemhlabeni. Siyafunda kwimimangaliso ayenzayo nakwindlela athi enze ngayo kwabanye. Singazama ukubufanisa obethu ubudlelane nabanye nokuhla kwaKhe okukhulu nokuhlala kwaKhe ngokuzithandela ukuze ananise uzuko lwasezulwini ngomkhombe (isifundo esingako esiza kuthi!). Kwelinye icala, ihlabathi lithe rhoqo ukusimema ukuba siziphakamise, siqhayise ngoko sikwenzileyo. Emkhumbini waseBhetelehem nakwiTitshala eyiNkosi, sifunda isifundo esahlukileyo—sokuba, umsebenzi kaThixo wemfundo nosindiso awufezwa ngokuziphakamisa thina kodwa ngokuzithoba phambi kukaThixo nokuba ngabakhonzi kwabanye.

`Ithini imeko ojongene nayo, nangoku, apho ukuzithoba kwakho kunokukunika ithuba elinamandla lokutyhila uYesu kwabanye?`
