“Uthando, isisekelo sendalo kanye nokuhlengwa, yisisekelo semfundo yeqiniso. Lokhu kwenziwe kwaba sobala emthethweni uNkulunkulu awunikeze njengomholi wempilo. Umthetho wokuqala nomkhulu yilo, ‘Woyithanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wakho ngayo yonke inhliziyo yakho, nangawo wonke umphefumulo wakho, nangawo onke amandla akho, nangayo yonke ingqondo yakho.’ Luka 10:27. Ukumthanda Yena, owaphakade owazi konke, ngamandla onke, nangengqondo, nangenhliziyo, Kuchaza ukuthuthuka okuphezulu kunakho konke kwamandla onke. Kusho ukuthi kuso sonke isiqu—umzimba, ingqondo, kanye nomphefumulo—umfanekiso kaNkulunkulu kumele uvuselelwe. “Njengowokuqala unjalo umthetho wesibili—“Uyakuthanda umakhelwane njengalokhu uzithanda wena.’ Mathewu 22:39. Umthetho wothando ubizela ekunikeleni umzimba, ingqondo, nomphefumulo ekusebenzeleni uNkulunkulu nabantu. Futhi lokhu kusebenza, ngenkathi kusenza isibusiso kwabanye, kuletha isibusiso esikhulu kunazo zonke kithina. Ukungabi nabugovu kuyisisekelo sakho konke ukuthuthuka kwangempela. Ngokusebenza okungesibo ubugovu samukela impucuko ephakeme yazo zonke izigaba. Siya ngokuba nesabelo sesimo esingcwele. Senziwa sifanele Izulu, ngokuba samukele Izulu ezinhliziyweni zethu.”—Ellen

G. White, Education, p. 16.
