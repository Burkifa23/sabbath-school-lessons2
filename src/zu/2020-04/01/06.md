---
title:  Abadelela Igunya
date:  01/10/2020
---

Abanye abantu bathathwa “njengabafundi ngokwemvelo” egumbini lokufundela. Abadingi nokutadisha ukuze baphumelele kakhulu. Bamukela ulwazi kalula. Ulwazi lwabo lubukeka “lunamathela.” UPetro wesibili 1 no 2, nokho, ukuveza obala ukuthi imfundo yethu kuKristu iyithuba lamava alinganayo kulabo abazozinikela. Amazwi akhuthazayo ka-2 Petro 1 aqhathaniswa nesixwayiso esiphusile sika-2 Petro 2.

`Funda u-2 Peter 2:1–17. Yimaphi amazwi anamandla nagwebayo awasho lapha na? Kusenjalo, phakathi kwalesisixwayiso nesigwebo esiqinileyo, yiliphi ithemba elikhulu esithenjiswa lona na?`

Qaphela lokho okubhalwa nguPetro kwindimana 10 mayelana nalabo abadelela igunya. Ngukukhuza okuqinile kwalokho esibhekene nako nanamuhla. Thina njengebandla kumele sisebenze ngokubuka amazinga athile egunya (buka uHeb. 13:7, 17, 24), futhi sibizelwe ekuzinikeleni nasekuwalaleleni, okungenani kangangokuba ethembekile wona kuNkulunkulu. Nakuba kunjalo, phakathi kokugweba okuqinile, uPetro unikeza (kundimana 9) iphuzu eliphikisayo. Uthi nakuba uNkulunkulu enamandla okuchitha labo abakhethe inkohliso, “uNkulunkulu uyakwazi ukophula abangcwele ezilingweni” (NKJV). Kungenzeka yini ukuthi ingxenye yemfundo yethu ebuKristwini akukona nje ukugwema izilingo kepha futhi nokufunda izindlela eziningi uNkulunkulu akwazi futhi asihlenga ngazo kuzo futhi nokusisiza siphephe kuzo, uyaxwayisa, “uyakubhusisa izimfundisoze ngesinyelela” (2 Pet.2:1, NKJV) na? Futhi, njengoba ukwedelela abaphathi kugxijwa kangaka, akumele yini imfundo yobuKristu ifake nokufunda indlela elungile yokuqonda, ukuzinikela, kanye nokuthobela “labo ababusa phezu kwenu” (Heb. 13:7, NKJV) na?

Nakuba othile engeke asho ukuthi uAdamu noEva badelele igunya, ekugcineni bakhetha ukungalaleli lelogunya. Okwenza isono sabo sibe sibi kakhulu ngukuthi bakwenza ngokuphikisana ngqo nalokho lelogunya, uNkulunkulu ngoKwakhe, ayebatshele kona, futhi ekwenzele ubuhle babo, ngokunjalo.

`Gxila kakhulu embuzweni wegunya, hhayi kuphela ebandleni noma emndenini, kepha empilweni ngokubanzi. Kungani igunya, kokubili ukusetshenziswa kwalo ngokufanele kanye nokuzinikela phansi kwalo ngokufanele, kusemqoka kangaka na? Woza nempendulo yakho eklasini ngeSabatha.`
