Genesis 3, Mshumayeli 2:18–23, Efesu 6:5–8; Read Ellen G. White, “The Temptation and Fall,” pp. 52–62, in Patriarchs and Prophets. Umsebenzi—isiqalekiso noma isibusiso na? Kwabukeka sengathu kweza njengengxenye yesono (Gen. 3:17). Ukufundisisa kwembula ukuthi ngumhlaba owaqalekiswa, hhayi umsebenzi. U-Ellen G. White uthi uNkulunkulu wayehlose ukuthi lokhu kwenza kusebenze njengesibusiso: “Impilo yokukhandleka kanye nokunaka okwakuzothi kusukela manje ibe yingxenye yomuntu yasekwa ngothando. Kwakuyisehlwayo esasidingekile ngenxa yesono sakhe, ukuvimbela ukuzitika kwenkanuko kanye nemizwa, ukuthuthukisa imikhuba yokuzithiba. Kwakuyingxenye yecebo elikhulu likaNkulunkulu lokululama komuntu ekulimaleni nasekonakeleni kwesono.”—Patriarchs and Prophets, p. 60. Kungenzeka yini ukuba siwenze waba yisiqalekiso ngokudina kwawo, ngokweqisa, noma ngokwenza indima yawo ibe ngaphezu kokuba iyiyo ezimpilweni zethu na? Nakuba sinjani isimo, kumele sifunde ukubeka umsebenzi endaweni yawo efanele. Futhi imfundo yobuKristu kumele isize ekuqeqesheni abantu ukuba bafunde inani lomsebenzi, Kusenjalo bangawenzi isithixo.

**Imibuzo Yokuxoxisana**

`1. Funda uMshumayeli 2:18-24. USolomoni angawubuka kanjani umsebenzi njengesibusiso kanye nesiqalekiso esigabeni esisodwa seBhayibheli na? Yiziphi izinkomba kulesisahluko mayelana nalokho okwenza umehluko ngendlela esibhekana ngayo nomsebenzi na?`

`2. Kungomsebenzi lapho sinakekela (sondla) imindeni yethu. Singawudlulisela kanjani umoya ofanele mayelana nomsebenzi emimndenini yethu na?`

`3. Umugca phakathi kokwenza umsebenzi obuqabavu kanye nokuba ngosebenza kuze kweqe ngezinye izikhathi muncane. Sizigcina kanjani ukuba singaweqi na? Buka uMshum. 2:23.`

`4. UPawule ukusho ngokusobala: “Ngokuba nalapho sisekini, saniyala ngalokhu ukuthi: “Uma umuntu engathandi ukusebenza, makangadli” (2 Thes. 3:10, NKJV). Nembala lesisimiso senza umqondo omkhulu. Kungaba Yisiphi isibonelo lapho singasebenzi khona na? Okungukuthi, kungani kumele siqinisekise ukungasenzi umthetho oqinileyo okungamele weqiwe na?`
