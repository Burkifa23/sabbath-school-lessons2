### Kuleliviki Funda
Gen. 3:1–15, 2 Cor. 4:6, Luke 1:26–38, Matt. 1:18–24, Eph. 4:15, 1 John 3:18, Deuteronomy 6.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “Ndodana yami, yizwa ukulaya kukayihlo, ungawushiyi umthetho kanyoko” (Proverbs 1:8, NKJV).

Njengesintu, siyafunda ngaso sonke isikhathi. Empeleni, impilo ngokwayo iyisikole. “Kusukela ezikhathini zokuqala abathembekile KwaIsrayeli babecophelele kakhulu emfundweni yentsha. INkosi yayikhombe ukuthi kusukela ebuncaneni abantwana kumele bafundiswe ngokulunga Kwayo kanye nobukhulu Bayo, ikakhulu njengoba kwembulwe emthethweni Wayo, futhi kwabonakala emlandweni kaIsrayeli. Amaculo kanye nokukhuleka kanye nezifundo zeZibhalo Kwakumele kulungiselelwe umqondo osavuleka. Obaba kanye nomama Kwakumele baqeqeshe izingane zabo ngokuthi umthetho kaNkulunkulu ubonisa Isimilo Sakhe, nokuthi lapho bamukela izimiso zomthetho ezinhliziyweni, umfanekiso kaNkulunkulu wawudwetshwa emqondweni nasemphefumulweni. Ukufundisa okuningi kwakungokukhuluma; kepha intsha yayifunda futhi Ukufunda izibhalo zesiHeberu; imiqulu yeTestamente Elidala yayivulelekile ukuba bayifunde.”—Ellen G. White, The Desire of Ages, p. 69.

Emlandweni omningi wesintu, imfundo yenzeke kakhulu ekhaya, ikakhulu eminyakeni yokuqala. Lithini iBhayibheli mayelana nemfundo emndenini, futhi yisiphi isimiso esingazithathela sona kulo, nakuba isimo somndeni wethu sinjani na?

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, October 10._
