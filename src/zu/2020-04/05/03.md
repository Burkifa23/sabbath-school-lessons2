---
title:  Ukwembula UBaba (Cont.)
date:  26/10/2020
---

Kwisandulela esihlaba umxhwele kwiVangeli lakhe (Johane 1:1-18), uJohane ukhuluma ngoJesu njenge “Zwi” laphakade. Imibango kaJohane ngoJesu ayihlonizi futhi ayinamkhawulo; ineqholo futhi ibanzi izwe lonke. UJesu wayekhona ngaphambi kokuba umhlaba ubekhona—kusukela ephakadeni, empeleni. Eqinisweni, uJesu kuNgaye ukudala (Johane 1:2, 3). “Ungukukhanya kwabo bonke abantu” (Johane 1:4, NRSV), futhi, njengoLizwi oweza emhlabeni, “ukhanyisela bonke” (Johane 1:9, NRSV).

`NgokukaJohane, uyini umphumela wokuba ngumuntu kukaKristu na? NjengoLizwi, yikuphi ukukhanya akuletha na? Yikuphi ukufaneleka anakho ukuba enze lokho na? Johane 1:14, 18.`

“Ukukhanya kwaqhamuka ngenkathi ubumnyama bomhlaba bujule kunakho konke. . . . “Lalilinye ithemba lesintu . . . lokuthi ukwazi ngoNkulunkulu kubuyiselwe emhlabeni. “UKristu weza ukuzoletha lololwazi. Weza ukuzochitha imfundisoze okungayo laba ababethi bayamazi uNkulunkulu babengammelanga ngokufanele. Weza ukuzofeza isimo somthetho Wakhe, Ukwembula ngesimilo Sakhe ubuhle bobungcwele.”—Ellen G. White, Education, pp. 74-76. Konke uJesu akwenza empilweni Yakhe emhlabeni kwakunenhloso eyodwa: “isambulo sikaNkulunkulu ukuze kuphakame isintu.”—Education, p. 82.

`UJesu ngoKwakhe uthi, “ ‘Noma ngubani obone mina ubone uBaba’ ” (Johane 14:9, NRSV). Kwakwenzenjani uJesu esho lamazwi na? Kungani awasho na? Johane 14:1– 14.`

Kulula ukusola inkulumo kaFiliphu ebhimbayo (Johane 14:8). Emuva kweminyaka yobudlelwane obusondelene noJesu, usageja iphuzu elisemqoka lukuguquka kukaJesu abengumuntu—ukuthi uJesu uzele ukuzoveza Isimilo sikaBaba. Mhlawumbe othisha namuhla bangathola induduzo eqinisweni lokuthi omunye wabafundi bakaThisha Omkhulu akenzanga kahle! Inkulumo kaFiliphu mhlawumbe ilotshelwe, nokho, hhayi ukunikeza isizathu sokumsola kepha ukunikeza thina ithuba lokuzihlola. Lide kangakanani ibanga osulihambe noJesu na? Futhi ngabe simqondile uJesu kangcono kunoFiliphu na? “ ‘Noma ngubani obone mina ubone uBaba.’ ”
