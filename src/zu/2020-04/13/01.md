### Funda Kuleliviki
Johane 3:16, 1 Johane 5:13, 1 Tim. 1:16, 1 Kor. 13:12, Zech. 13:6.

> <p>Indimana Yekhanda</p>
> “ ‘Lokho iso elingakubonanga, nendlebe engakuzwanga, okungavelanga enhliziyweni yamuntu, uNkulunkulu akulungisela abamthandayo’ ” (1 Korinte 2:9, NKJV).

Iombongi, yesaba ukufa, yabuza ukuthi umuntu angaphila kanjani ngaphandle “kokwazi ngokuqinisekile ukuthi kusa kuni, kufa kuni, siphetho sini, okwakulindelekile ngale kwethuna na?” Waloba enkondlweni yakhe lokho akubiza nge-IHP, “Institute of Preparation for the Hereafter.” Kepha othile angalungiselela kanjani ingale kwethuna uma engazi ukuthi kwenzekani emuntwini kulo na? ngenhlanhla, iBhayibheli lisinikeza ukhasha olukhulu ngodaba lwezulu, umhlaba omusha, kanye nokufunda Nokuphila esizokwenza kulo lonke iphakade. Njengoba sibonile kuyo yonke lekota, i-IHP ilapha futhi njengamanje, kulokhu kuphila, futhi yonke imfundo yethu—nakuba ikomuphi umkhakha—kumele isilungiselele lelokusasa.

Kusenjalo, noma yisiphi isikole singadlulisela ulwazi oluningi olulungileyo, ulwazi oluningi oluhle noluphathekayo futhi nolusizayo. Kodwa kumenzelani okuhle umuntu uma engazuza lonke lolo lwazi kepha alahlekelwe ngukuphila okuphakade na? Kuleliviki sizobuka lokho esikutshelwa nguMoya ngesikole sokugcina semfundo, isikole siqhubeka ingunaphakade futhi lapho sizofunda futhi sikhule ingunaphakade. Kulesisikole sangale kwethuna, sizofunda izinto, kulomhlaba wamanje, esingakwazi nokuzicabanga.

_*Funda isifundo saleliviki ukulungiselela iSabatha, December 26._
