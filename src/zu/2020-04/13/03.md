---
title:  Ukuphila Okusha
date:  21/12/2020
---

`“Futhi uNkulunkulu uyakuzesula izinyembezi zonke emehlweni abo; ukufa akusayikuba khona; nokudabuka, nokukhala, nobuhlungu akusayikuba khona; ngokuba okokuqala kudlulile” (Samb. 21:4). Lokhu kusitshelani mayelana nendlela okwehluke ngayo kunalomhlaba Ukuphila kwethu Okusha okuyoba yiko, Ukuphila okungenako ukufa, usizi, kanye nobuhlungu na?`

UmKristu wayekhuluma nomngani mayelana nethemba leVangeli, isithembiso sempilo engunaphakade ngoJesu Krsitu. Lomuntu waphendula ngokungekuhle kulokho. “Impilo engunaphakade na?” kusho yena ngokuthuthumela. “Ngumcabango omubi kakhulu! Iminyaka yethu engu-70-80 lapha mibi ngokwanele. Ngubani ongafuna ukunweba lokhu ingunaphakade na? Kungaba yisihogo lokho.” Lomuntu ubengaba nephuzu, ngaphandle kokuthi wayengaqondi ukuthi isithembiso sempilo engunaphakade akukona nje ukuqhubeza impilo esinayo lapha. Maye—ngubani obengafuna lokho na? Kunalokho, njengoba indimana engenhla isho, okudala sekwedlulile, futhi zonke izinto sezizintsha.

**Izindimana ezilandelayo zisitshelani mayelana Nokuphila Okusha okuzayo na?**

`2 Pet. 3:10–13`

`Samb. 21:1–6`

`Umbuzo obalulekileyo kithina kukho konke lokhu yilo: Kuthatha ini ukuba yingxenye yalokhu kuphila Okusha na? Sifinyelela kanjani lapho na? Singaqinisekisa kanjani ukuthi sizoba yingxenye yako na? Yiziphi izinto empilweni yethu, uma zikhona, ezingasivimbela ekubeni yingxenye yalokho uNkulunkulu asithembise kona ngoJesu na?`
